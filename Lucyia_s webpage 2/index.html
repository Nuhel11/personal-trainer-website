About Me

Hi, I’m Lucy Atzori, a certified personal trainer.
I started my fitness journey in 2021, feeling self-conscious and unsure every time I stepped into the gym.
Everything changed after surgery made me realize the incredible power of strength training and proper nutrition for recovery and overall health.
Inspired by my experience, I dove deep into learning and earned my certification to help others discover the same transformative benefits.
Fitness has reshaped my life, guiding me toward a healthier, more confident version of myself.
Now, my passion is to help women, LIKE YOU, achieve meaningful transformations in both health and lifestyle.
I’m here to offer you a path, purpose, and unwavering support as you embark on your own fitness journey!
